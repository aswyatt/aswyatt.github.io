About Me

I'm a scientific software developer with a focus on high-performance simulations, interactive visualizations, and robust, reproducible workflows. My work specializes in numerical modeling of ultrashort optical pulses — integrating advanced FFT-based techniques, GPU acceleration, and precision algorithm design.

I bring a pragmatic approach to development: modular Python architectures, rigorous unit testing, and Docker-based pipelines to ensure consistent performance across environments. I'm equally comfortable optimizing a spectral axis for physical fidelity as I am designing intuitive PySide6 GUIs for usability and insight.

Beyond core simulations, I value clean interfaces and exploratory tools — whether that's a real-time sketch written in P5.js or an elegant frontend for scientific analysis.
